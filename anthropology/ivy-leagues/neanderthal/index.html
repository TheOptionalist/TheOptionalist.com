Neanderthals: Ivy League Reading Notes

A higher-order synthesis focusing on comparative frameworks and historiography.

Key Points

  • Contrast biological adaptation with cultural innovation.
  • Map debates: replacement vs assimilation vs continuity.
  • Evaluate evidence: fossils, tools, genetics, paleoclimate.
  • Use interdisciplinary sources (genetics + archaeology).
  • Highlight methodological limits and uncertainty.

Exam Prompts

  • Compare at least two explanatory models for Neanderthal extinction.
  • How do genetic studies reshape earlier fossil interpretations?
  • Discuss the limits of archaeological inference.

Glossary

  • Assimilation: Replacement with limited interbreeding.
  • Historiography: Study of how scholarship has evolved.
  • Paleoclimate: Reconstruction of ancient climate.
